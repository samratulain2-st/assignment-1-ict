About Us

Shahi Dastarkhwan is a celebration of rich tradition and authentic desi flavors,bringing together the essence of royal diningto your table.Inspired by the grandeur of Mughlai cuisine,our restaurant offers a wide variety of delicious dishes, from aromatic biryaniand flavorfull karahi to freshly baked naan and mouthwatering desserts.Every meal is prepared with high quality ingredientsand traditional spices to ensure an unforgettable taste experience.
